About ReWear

ReWear is more than just a platform - it's a small step toward a greener and kinder world.

We believe that fashion doesn't have to be wasteful. Every year, millions of clothes are thrown away while others go without. ReWear was created to change that.

Our goal is simple: make it easy for people to exchange clothes they no longer wear and give them a second life. Whether you're swapping directly with someone or using your points to grab a new outfit, you're helping reduce waste and support sustainable fashion.

We're a community that believes in sharing, in caring for the planet, and in making small changes that matter. Everyone deserves to feel good — and do good — without spending big.

"The world doesn't need a few people doing sustainability perfectly. It needs millions doing it imperfectly."

Thank you for being a part of this journey. Let's ReWear, not re-buy.

- The ReWear Team
